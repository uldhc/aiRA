Par aiRA

aiRA rīku ir izveidojis Latvijas Universitātes Digitālo humanitāro zinātņu centrs projekta ȬPEN ietvaros (Nr. ZDA-LIP 2025/2). Projekts veicina zināšanu pieejamību un sabiedrības iesaisti zināšanu radīšanā. Šis rīks ir atvērtā koda projekts un pieejams GitHub.

How it works

The user selects a task, chooses an AI provider and model, enters an API key, uploads images, and then generates descriptions or transcriptions.

What you can do

  • Generate alt text for uploaded images.
  • Transcribe visible text from images.
  • Translate generated descriptions into another language.
  • Edit, review, and export the final results.

Privacy note

When generating results, uploaded images and prompts are sent to the selected AI provider. Do not upload sensitive images unless this is allowed by your organization.
